How To Create Gate.io Account

How to Register a Gate.io Account

1: Go to Gate.io website and click on the “Sign Up” button on top right.

Create Gate.io Account

2: Register with email

Choose your country or region. Input username, your email address and password. Please check “I certify that I am 18 years of age or older, and I agree to the Gate.io User Agreement Privacy Policy” and click “NEXT”.

3: Set your fund password and click “Create account”. Please note that your fund password contains at least 6 characters and can not be the same as your login password.

An activation email will be sent to your email address. Complete the rest of the registration process following the instructions in the email to activate your account. Once its done, click “Email activated, please log in”.
